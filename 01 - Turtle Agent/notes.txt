Turtle Trading is the idea that everything relies on the trends in the market. Turtle trading persists over time and that you can capture profits
using such strategy. 

It is the simplest trading strategy but one of the most fundamental. 

Moving Averages

- We use Moving Averages to calculate the maximum or minimum within a specific trend of the entire market. For instance, we can utilize maximum 
values to know when the current trend is going to drop or minimum values to know when it is going to peak.
